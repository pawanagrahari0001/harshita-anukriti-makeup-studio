The Artist Behind the Studio

Harshita Anukriti is a professionally trained makeup artist and educator, known for creating elegant, timeless bridal looks that enhance natural beauty rather than overpower it.

With years of hands-on experience in bridal makeup, event styling, and professional training, she brings a calm, personalized, and detail-oriented approach to every client.

From hygiene standards to premium products and modern techniques, every step is thoughtfully curated to ensure comfort, confidence, and flawless results.

Our Philosophy

Makeup is not about changing how you look — it’s about enhancing who you are.

We believe in personalized looks, skin-friendly products, hygienic practices, and techniques that stand the test of time, lights, and lenses.
